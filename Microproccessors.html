Microprocessors

The fourth generation of computers is Microproccessors. Thousands of intricate circuits can be built onto only one silicon chip. This technology had become so advance.

Due to the advance in this technology, these computers became affordable to the middle class man. This generation also led to the discovery of graphical user interfaces a.k.a. GUI, the mouse and many other handheld computers.
